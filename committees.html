Committees

CRISED

The Commission for Research and Implementation of Sustainable Energy Development focuses on the development of a sustainable future by accelerating research and using present resources optimally. It also looks into the capacity of current research projects and aims to execute and implement them properly for the best output with justified economic implications.

Know More

IP

The International Press is unlike any other committee. Consisting of a group of reporters and journalists, this committee becomes the eyes and ears of the public. The reporters record and report the proceedings of other committees, hold the delegations responsible for their actions in committee and seek no bias towards any country. They publish articles, hold interviews and press conferences. This team brings forth the truth and justifies the need for media and press in decision-making for the future.

Know More

FECC

The Futuristic Energy Crisis Committee focuses on devising solutions and applications to avert the impending energy crisis. It involves searching for the best means to save our future while securing our resources. It also judges the impact it may have on human needs and survival; and the future in its entirety.

Know More

CESC

The Council for Environmental Sustainability and Conservation prioritizes a sustainable future. With the resources we currently have and the constantly rising threat of an environmental crisis, this council emphasizes and aims to achieve the sustainability goals and conserve the environment with utmost urgency.

Know More

ICRED

Pertaining specifically to Indian energy needs, the Indian Council for Renewable Energy Development discusses the prospects of the development and implementation of renewable energy. It also focuses on bringing a significant change in carbon emission patterns and foresees a clean energy-based future for India.
